Speakers

Chris Coyier
Chris is a web designer and developer

A Front-end ninja. He created and runs CSS-Tricks, a website all about building websites, now going strong for 10 years.

Whitney Homans
Whitney is a Senior Design Manager at Mailchimp

She started the UX Content Strategy discipline at Mailchimp in 2017 and has grown it into a team of 7 that works cross-functionally among Mailchimp's product areas.

Phil Hawksworth
Phil is Principal Developer Experience Engineer at Netlify.

With a passion for browser technologies, and the empowering properties of the web, he loves seeking out ingenuity and simplicity, especially in places where over-engineering is common.

Eva Ferraira
Evangelina Ferreira is a Front-end developer and teacher.

She currently works as a UI Developer and has been teaching web technologies at the National Technological University of Argentina for more than five years.

Aaron Walter
VP of Design Education at InVision

Aarron Walter draws upon 15 years of experience running product teams and teaching design to help companies enact design best practices.

Cassidy Williams
Cassidy is an instructor and developer with React Training

She's worked for CodePen, Amazon, L4 Digital, Clarifai, and Venmo, and she's had the honor of working with various organizations, including cKeys and Hacker Fund as their Director of Outreach.
